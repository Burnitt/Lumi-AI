# Lumi-AI
The objective of Lumi-AI is to help SMB(small/medium Businesses) to market there product throught the idea of Campaigns over a period of time

Ways to market:
- Instagram, Facebook (Fully Automatic for pictures / Client provides us with content to upload)
- X (Twitter) (Fully Automatic for pictures / Client provides us with content to upload)
- LinkedIn (Fully Automatic for pictures / Client provides us with content to upload)
- TikTok (Client provides us with content to upload)
- Email Marketing (Fully Automatic)
- Ad Platforms (Fully Automatic)

Campaigns: 
Campaigns work on the idea of marketing one idea or product for the client. 
- Each campaign can last from 2wks --> 6mon, we provide them with: 2wk, 1mon, 3mon, 6mon (Standard Pricing)
- Each has X targets they must reach (e.g. 20% more interaction on post, 15% more SEO recognition)

An integral part of this is updates: 
- Post performances
- Weekly summaries on growth
- Approval on each post
- Approval on plan
